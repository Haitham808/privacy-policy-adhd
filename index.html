Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 30, 2026

1. Data Collection

Our app, ADHD Planner for Adults, is designed with privacy in mind. Your Brain Dump and Focus Screen data is stored locally on your device or in your personal iCloud account.

2. Third-Party Services

We use RevenueCat to manage subscriptions. RevenueCat receives anonymized transaction data to verify your $7.99 monthly or Lifetime Pro purchase. They do not have access to your tasks or notes.

3. Your Rights

Because we do not store your personal task data on our own servers, you have full control over your data. You can delete your data at any time by deleting the app or clearing your iCloud storage.
